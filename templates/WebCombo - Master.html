Never miss an inspection again with Schedules

Set one-off or recurring reminders for those day-to-day inspections with Schedules.

What are Schedules?

Inspection schedules are recurring reminders you can create and assign to your team members for regular inspections, such as asset maintenance checks. Set defined timeframes for each schedule so you and your team know exactly when to start and complete them.

Perfect for those inspections which are required on a daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly basis, or if you simply have an upcoming one that you don’t want to forget. You can even integrate schedules with your own calendar.

How do I create and update Schedules?

Inspection schedules can be created via the SafetyCulture web app. You must have the "schedule management" permission in order to create a new schedule. On the web app:

  1. Select Schedule from the menu on the left-hand side.
  2. Click + Schedule inspections on the upper-right corner of the page.
  3. Configure the schedule accordingly.
  4. Click Create. If the schedule is assigned to more than 20 users, click Create schedule in the pop-up window.
Can I integrate Schedules into my calendar?

Yes! Your inspection schedules can be synced to your supported calendar app of choice in just a few clicks. Your calendar can only be integrated via the web app.

Once you've set up the integration, your calendar will:

  • Show inspection schedules that are assigned to you as events, up to one month in advance.
  • Detail each calendar event with a link that allows you to start the relevant inspection via both the web app and the mobile app.
  • Hide past schedule occurrences.

Each calendar app refreshes their calendar subscriptions at different rates. Depending on the calendar app you use, inspection schedules that have just been assigned to you may take some time to appear. For steps on how to integrated schedules with your calendar, see this page.
